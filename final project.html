Final Project Randon Tennis

My project is about a fun game where the objective is to get 10 points before the other player does. It's tennis, but a little more random, meaning anyone can win!

I created everything about this game on my own. To move the top player, hold "W, A, S, D". To move the bottom player, hold "up, down, left, right". To reset the game, hold "r"
